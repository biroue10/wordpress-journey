A public, day-by-day log of my hands-on journey to become a **WordPress expert**.

I'm documenting everything in the open — what I build, what I break, what I learn —
as I work toward a **Happiness Engineer** role at [Automattic](https://automattic.com/work-with-us/).
This repo is my proof of work: real projects, real notes, real progress.

**Maintainer:** Isaac Biroue · [@biroue10](https://github.com/biroue10)

---

## Why this repo exists

- **Consistency over intensity.** A daily log beats a weekend cram. Every entry is dated.
- **Learning by building.** Plugins, themes, WP-CLI, WooCommerce, MySQL — hands on, not just reading.
- **Working in the open.** If it's not documented, it didn't happen.
